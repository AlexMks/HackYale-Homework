Snapshots of Spain

Nerja

Our journey through Spain began in Nerja, a cliffside city on the southern coast of Spain full of white-walled dwellings and narrow streets. It is touristy, but to understand Spain you must understand its tourism; the recreation of foreigners accounts for a sizable portion of its economy.

La Alhambra

"How unworthy is my scribbling of the place," wrote American author Washington Irving. He wrote of Granada, Spain - location of La Alhambra, a Moorish palace and citadel that has captured literary imagination since before the Golden Age of Spanish Literature.

Leon

The majority of my time in Spain found me in the northwestern city of Leon, a small town where I lived with a host-family and took classes at the local university. The city, a quieter locale relatively unknown to tourists, served as an ideal homebase for travels throughout northern Spain.
