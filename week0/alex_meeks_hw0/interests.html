Things I Love

Ballroom Dancing

The Yale Ballroom Dance Team has seen me through all four years at Yale. After joining without any dance experience, I now compete at the Gold-level in Latin and Rhythm! I also served as Treasurer of the team my junior year, which taught me about accounting for and helping coordinate a large organization. My partner and I travel around the Northeast with the rest of the team to compete, and occasionally perform choreographed pieces at Yale. The world of ballroom is fascinating, addicting to watch, and has its own fair share of drama.
